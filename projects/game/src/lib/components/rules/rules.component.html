Object

Move all your colored marbles up the board to the opposite triangle.

Moves

Players take turns moving one marble in one of six possible directions, one space at a time, to an unoccupied space.

Jumping

A marble may jump an adjacent marble of any color in a straght line if the immediate space beyond is unoccupied. The jumping piece (and only the jumping piece) may continue to make similar jumps (in in any diirection) during the same turn. Jumping is not compulsary, and the jumped pieces are not moved nor removed from the board. All pieces remain in play for the entire game, except those of a player who resigns.

Jerry's Jump Multiplier

Jerry's Jump Multiplier allows a marble to jump a marble any number of spaces away as long as it can land the same number of spaces away on the far side and there are no other marbles in between on either side.

Time for some ASCII art. Say @ is your jumping marble, O is any other marble, _ is an empty space, and x is the empty destination space. The following are legal jumps:
@ O x
@ _ O _ x
@ _ _ O _ _ x
@ _ _ _ O _ _ _ x
@ _ _ _ _ O _ _ _ _ x
@ _ _ _ _ _ O _ _ _ _ _ x
etc.

The following are not legal jumps:
@ _ O x
@ _ O _ _ x
@ _ _ _ O _ O _ x
@ _ O _ _ O _ _ _ _ x

When making multiple jumps, how you jumped the first marble does not affect how you must jump the second. The following is a legal multiple jump:
@ _ O _ x _ _ O _ _ x O x
