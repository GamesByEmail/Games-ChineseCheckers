Jerry's Jump Multiplier.

Jerry's Jump Multiplier allows a marble to jump another marble any number of spaces away as long as it can land the same number of spaces away on the far side and there are no other marbles in between on either side. In the examples below, @ can jump O to land on empty x as long as all _ are empty.

  • @ O x
  • @ _ O _ x
  • @ _ _ O _ _ x
  • @ _ _ _ O _ _ _ x
  • @ _ _ _ _ O _ _ _ _ x
  • @ _ _ _ _ _ O _ _ _ _ _ x
